Regional Investment Allocation Decision

Poplar Point vs Tysons Central vs Project Connect

Overview

Three development sites were evaluated using financial return, market risk, and public value.

Final decision: Poplar Point is selected due to highest long-term public value despite lower financial return.

Financial Comparison

Site Base RLV Financial Strength
Project Connect $47.6M Strongest
Tysons Central $24.3M Moderate
Poplar Point $5.5M Weakest

Sensitivity Findings (Risk)

Project Connect is the most resilient under market stress. Poplar Point is the most sensitive to downside risk (rent drops, cost increases, and higher interest rates).

Policy Comparison

  • Poplar Point: Highest equity impact and waterfront activation
  • Project Connect: Strengthens existing innovation corridor
  • Tysons Central: Incremental suburban redevelopment

Final Recommendation

Invest in Poplar Point.

Despite a ~$42M opportunity cost in residual land value versus Project Connect and ~$19M versus Tysons Central, Poplar Point delivers the highest long-term public value and structural regional impact.
